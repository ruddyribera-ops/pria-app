The textbook uses `> EN ESTA UNIDAD:` as the primary unit delimiter. To automatically detect units:

1. Search for `EN ESTA UNIDAD` pattern
2. Extract the CONVERSEMOS section that follows (contains unit objectives)
3. Map page numbers from the OCR (page appears at end of content block)
4. Identify main reading text from "Antes de la lectura" section
